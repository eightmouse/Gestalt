:::note Why, Nomura?
I don't have much to say here, I had not intention of buying a whole new console for this game and I did not.
I watched a full play through on youtube and called it a day.

I have to admit, gameplay was fun and it used the 3DS tech + touch screen and dual screen, in a clever way.. however it was once again a slap in the face from Nomura, releasing very important plot info into another console, AGAIN.

As I said, gameplay wise it was fun.. plot was interesting and quite important to understand everything before and after.
I yet have to play this game, I'm sure I will as I have the steam collection but I don't plan on doing it anytime soon.
All I have to say is that, I was very disappointed back then as I am now, I'll probably carry it with me forever.
:::
