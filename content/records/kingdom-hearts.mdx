First time I played Kingdom Hearts was.. when I got my Playstation 2, I remember buying it in bundle with a Platinum version of Kingdom Hearts.
![kingdom-hearts](/media/records/kingdom-hearts/1778822248285-dsc-0392-1.jpg)
It's hard for me to recommend Kingdom Hearts, not because of this title specifically but the sequels that turned it into a mess.
Personally, I think you should be able to play the main line ones and be completely fine.. however, you'll be missing on context and start wondering 'What is going on?'.

What is Kingdom Hearts?
Kingdom Hearts at the core is a JRPG, very simple (and clean) that works very well and its addicting due to all the skills, abilities and modifiers available.
I recommend playing the 'Final Mix' version, not necessarily for the extra content but for the positive changes/fixes.
![kingdom-hearts](/media/records/kingdom-hearts/1778823161229-0wwswvp6nci41.jpg)

For me personally and I'm assuming for a lot of people too, it struck because of the Disney connection.
So you have an adventure JRPG game, with cute/cartoonish style mixed with Disney popular characters, that has to be a hit.. right?
Well yes, It works very well and the problems are not related to the art style used, music or anything technical really.. it stems more from a plot perspective that make it hard to follow in the spin-offs and sequels.
![kingdom-hearts](/media/records/kingdom-hearts/1778823381391-maxresdefault.jpg)

Why?
Time travel, it's introduced later on in other titles and because of that, I struggle to recommend Kingdom Hearts fully.. because once you play and finish the first one, you'll obviously be starved and looking for more.

Don't get me wrong.. the game is good, even with the plot flaws I still like it and I think majority of people like it too, it's just a love and hate scenario.
If you're someone that doesn't look too hard into things, then go ahead and enjoy one of the best thing that ever happened to Playstation 2 and Square Enix in general.
Otherwise be ready to spend time digging for infos online and argue with people about theories and whatnot.
