:::note A scattered memory that's like a far off dream.
This is another one of the spin-offs that came out for a different platform, lots of people missed it.. I almost did too.
I won't google stuff, cause these notes have to be genuine.. so forgive me if timelines are messy.

This came out after the DS one, it was an exclusive for PSP and luckily for me, since I was a spoiled kid.. I owned one.
Complaints aside for being a spin-off, the game its very solid and reinforced what I felt for the DS title, a portable KH with better graphics, improved under every aspect (almost) but obviously still portable, so limits were there.
![kingdom-hearts-birth-by-sleep](/media/records/kingdom-hearts-birth-by-sleep/1778830005826-maxresdefault.jpg)

Plot wise it was obviously interesting as we finally get to know who the 3 fellas from the secret ending of KH2FM are, on top of that we get a bigger picture of the whole situation but it's also were it gets messier because of time travels and recurring familiar faces.
At this point, I knew Roxas from KH2 but why is there another Roxas in my PSP game?????????????????
The game does tell you, it's not Roxas but Ventus but this is also were things get trickier because somethings in these games are just subtle or not always explicitly told, which can be fine or a problem, depending on who plays it.
![kingdom-hearts-birth-by-sleep](/media/records/kingdom-hearts-birth-by-sleep/1778830017742-hq720.jpg)

This game is where I kinda started feeling the darker (no puns), melancholic side of the plot.
You get Terra, which struggles the whole time with his internal battle, figuring things out.
Aqua, being the mother of the trio.
Ventus, the little brother that is just not aware of what is going on and ends up being swallowed by this whole ordeal.. well kinda.
![kingdom-hearts-birth-by-sleep](/media/records/kingdom-hearts-birth-by-sleep/1778830054577-kingdom-hearts-birth-by-sleep-aqua-standing-in-the-station-of-awakening.jpg)

Frustrating that they had to hide each different ending behind their individual playthrough, cause past the differences in gameplay.. the game is repetitive, you're re-doing the same worlds in a different order but with a different character, that's it.
I did it, because I was not aware of it but I really don't see the point on recommending people to do it, unless you're aiming for 100%.
![kingdom-hearts-birth-by-sleep](/media/records/kingdom-hearts-birth-by-sleep/1778830066838-when-playing-birth-by-sleep-what-order-do-you-guys-play-v0-fkur6y5csma91.webp)

I liked all the various minigames, not all of them, NOT ALL OF THEM.
The introduction of more Disney world and characters is always a surprise, I think most people always look forward to a new title also because of that, you'll never know what worlds they introduce or if they'll show a different version to something we've already visited.

All that said, I liked it... it left something bitter but I liked it and honestly, being able to play a KH game wherever you wanted, was just too good.
OSTs impeccable as always.
:::
