:::note 20 / 05 / 2026 - What happened to KiraPatch?
Helloo.
KiraParch is alive and well, I've been working on in this whole time. I took some breaks to finish Gestalt so I can post more in-depth notes and rambles of what is going on with the stuff I'm working on.
KiraPatch is progressing, I've verified all of the games and having issues only with 2 so far and I can't just release an half update.
The big time consuming part is verifying that each Method is producing shinies, legitimately and that they are within odds. It's a boring process that requires lots of trial and errors and even tho I build an harness around it to speed up the process/help, it's not that easy.

Initially I was not planning on going full in and make the shiny '100%' legal, however I also feel like it was an half baked truth, so here we are now.

It's no possible to make a '100%  legal' shiny, in a sense that Pokemon can any day change the rules and make everything, including pkhex, obsolete or just 'tools'.
The goal here is to make shinies that are perfect in every aspect and that pkhex will always consider as legal, unless the aforementioned change by Pokemon.
Currently pkhex, checks for specific 'Paths', so when Pokemon are generated using pkhex, they're simulating a real encounter.. HOWEVER:
Under scrutiny, those pokemon can be indentified as genned and therefore illegal. So pkhex is not producing perfectly legal, unrecognizable pokemons.
We're basically all at the mercy of Pokemon, just accepting genned pokemon without doing a deep check.

What does this entail? So long as Pokemon themselves are okay with pokemon being genned, I can make patchers for all the different generations basically, so that people can experience shy hunting without having to deal with huge odds.. but at the same time getting 'legit' shinies.

NB:
Not throwing shades at pkhex, I just want to clarify cause many people are of the idea that genning Pokemon is 100% legal, safe etc etc.
It isn't and the devs of pkhex know too, no devs deals in absolute.. those are sith- jokes aside..
pkhex is an amazing tool to make competitive ready teams/have fun!
