:::note 24 / 05 / 2026 - Madaramen't
Got little bit caught into some life stuff, so I had not lots of time to play. However I at least managed to finish Madarame's palace!
![Palace time](/media/records/persona-5-royal/1779634332388-screenshot-2026-05-24-222549.png)
![Finally](/media/records/persona-5-royal/1779634486665-screenshot-2026-05-24-223357.png)

Soo, after doing the calling card thing I accidentally wasted a day thinking I could've strengthen some bonds, oh well.... It's fine, I don't think it's going to affect anything, hopefully............
Anyway, I completely changed my mind on Madarame, Atlus is very good at making characters and so far I have not been disappointed.
This next section reminds me of Devil May Cry, don't know why.
![DEVIL MAY CRYYYY](/media/records/persona-5-royal/1779634630354-screenshot-2026-05-24-223637.png)

Going back to what I said about Madarame;
Unlike the first Palace where they kinda make the villain quite obvious or at least he's just an A-hole the whole time, so it can only get worse, with Madarame they made me feel 'Hmm, I think this time we might be wrong.. maybe its just a random old dude that does some despicable stuff but nothing wild' aaaaaaand I was wrong.
![This dude](/media/records/persona-5-royal/1779634785600-screenshot-2026-05-24-223736.png)

Why I am surprised is because Atlus did a good job at making, in a very short amount of time, a character that has a solid personality. In real life he's an innocent acting artist, that did some bad but it's justified somehow. Inside the palace, we find out that not only he plagiarized artworks, he killed someone to push his career further.
![fuck u](/media/records/persona-5-royal/1779634966080-screenshot-2026-05-24-223758.png)
![fuck u pt2](/media/records/persona-5-royal/1779635024698-screenshot-2026-05-24-223846.png)

Not only he stole Yusuke's mother painting, he left her to die. I was '0______________________0' bro.
Like, I don't know if there's going to be more evil stuff but I was not expecting this level of evil.. I was expecting a more 'child' frinedly type of evil, not some Shutter Island type of plot twist.
Anyway, the boss fight was pretty chill.. didn't really struggle that much although its probably thanks to me speeding last week grinding randomly some levels while testing and doing some other stuff.
![Coool](/media/records/persona-5-royal/1779635248887-screenshot-2026-05-24-223935.png)
It was a cool looking boss though, can't imagine what's at the end of the tunnel........
Also, another person has been mentioned.. I have no idea who that might be since there's no one else with Phantom Thievery powers, at least that I know of so far.

Next session I'll find out if Madarame had a change of heart or I need to actually kill him XD.
Also, funny how while we're being all emotional and listening to Yusuke opening up and stuff, Riujy comes back with a Fanta and starts burping and shizzzz
![Dude bro, have some manners](/media/records/persona-5-royal/1779635439227-screenshot-2026-05-24-224424.png)
These dynamic so far reminds me  a lot of One Piece, which is even more fitting considering Mona is LITERALLY CHOPPER.

Overall, good sesh~

:::note 18 / 05 / 2026 - Aight, I was wrong
![Look at Madarame goofy ahh](/media/records/persona-5-royal/1779106132693-screenshot-2026-05-17-225919.png)
Yeah I admit it, I was wrong..
They absolutely did a good job in making Madarame looking somewhat innocent, like I said in the previous note.. if the peak of this bs was just plagiarizing, doesn't really make that bad..
However, with all the new info gathered after getting into the palace and confronting Madarame, yep... he's quite something.

Now, for the crazy COOL looking part that I kinda expected but not fully, was not sure..
Yusuke, GETTING. A . PERSONA(?).
Like I said, I was half expecting/hoping for something like this but I kinda give up the idea cause I was like 'Nah, he's just one of the victim, we will help him and that's it"
![The coolest character so far, after Joker and Ann](/media/records/persona-5-royal/1779106403416-screenshot-2026-05-17-230300.png)

First off Yusuke's Persona reminds me of Oden, so that's already a 10 in my book.. on top of that Yusuke also looks crazy cool both aesthetically and personality wise after getting the persona.
I already put him on the team and play around with his ability a little, he's very cool, love it!

This session wasn't particularly long but surely packed ~

:::note 16 / 05 / 2026 - Madarame, no steal my art pls
